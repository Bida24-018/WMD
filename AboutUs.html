PitchWear is a business based in Francistown and Gaborone. We have 50 employees in both branches dedicated to providing you with the best quality football T-Shirts. These T-Shirts are bought directly from their respective teams. We then customize them for those who want customized T-Shirts. Whether you want your favourite player's name and number to be at the back of your T-Shirt or you want YOUR name written, we are more than ready to do that for you.

If you want to order our T-Shirts or raise a complaint if you not satisfied with anything, please visit out social meadia pages and send a message. We have social meadia teams awaiting to hear from you. We also deliver around the country.
We saw the need to sell T-Shirts to Batswana around the country who love their favourite teams and who want to wear their team's T-Shirt proudly without worried about them being of low quality.
